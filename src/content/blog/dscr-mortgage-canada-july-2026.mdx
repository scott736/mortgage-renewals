**DSCR loan Canada** searches are climbing — especially from investors who know the US non-QM product and want the Canadian equivalent.

## The short version

**DSCR = Net Operating Income ÷ Annual Debt Service.** Typical floors are **1.10–1.25**. Below 1.0, the property does not cover its own mortgage on paper.

## Canada ≠ US

US DSCR is a packaged non-QM consumer product. In Canada, DSCR-style lending sits mainly in **commercial, alternative, and portfolio** channels via specialist brokers — not a Big Six posted product.

## At renewal

Use DSCR when stacked GDS/TDS across multiple rentals fails A-lender math, or when personal income is thin but the property cash-flows. Model add-back vs DCR first with the [rental income qualifying calculator](/rental-income-qualifying-calculator/).
