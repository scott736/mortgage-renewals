A **1–4 unit rental** usually renews in the residential investor channel — not full commercial underwriting. Use this checklist 120 days out.

## Rate

- Get the incumbent investor renewal offer
- Ask for the **owner-occupied vs rental** quote gap (often ~0.10%–0.30%+)
- Shop monolines and banks through a broker

## Qualification

- Run **50% add-back**, **80% offset**, and **DCR** on the [rental income calculator](/rental-income-qualifying-calculator/)
- If stacked GDS/TDS fails across multiple doors, ask about [DSCR / portfolio](/dscr-mortgage-canada/) options

## Documents

- Current lease(s) and rent proof
- Property tax and insurance
- Mortgage statement / payout if switching
