Google Search Console shows strong demand for **“documents required for mortgage renewal”** — and confusion between stay-put renewals and lender switches.

## Stay with your lender

A straight renewal (same lender, no new money) is often just:

- Signed renewal agreement
- Photo ID if re-verified
- Updated PAD / void cheque if banking changed

## Switch or refinance

Expect a full package: income proof, mortgage statement/payout, property tax, insurance, credit consent, and sometimes an appraisal. Self-employed borrowers add T1s and NOAs; investors add leases or rent rolls.

## Timing

Start **120 days** before maturity if you might switch — that is when rate holds open and conditions can clear before payout.
