At **five or more residential units**, underwriting shifts toward rent rolls, NOI, and DSCR. That is also where **CMHC MLI Select** can change the deal.

## When MLI Select wins

- You need **higher LTV** than conventional commercial caps
- Longer **amortization** improves cash flow enough to justify the insurance premium
- The building scores on CMHC **affordability, energy, and accessibility** criteria

## When conventional commercial wins

- The asset will not score enough MLI Select points
- You need speed or a structure insurance will not support
- Sponsor/recourse terms are cleaner with a relationship commercial lender

Premium grids were updated in mid-2025 — verify current pricing with a multi-family specialist before you assume last year’s math.
