Queries like **commercial mortgage rates Canada** return bank product pages — not a transparent consumer rate sheet. That is by design.

## How commercial rates are set

Lenders quote a **spread over cost of funds**, then adjust for LTV, asset class, lease quality, recourse, and sponsor experience. Owner-occupied commercial can price tighter than pure investment assets.

## Do not paste residential rates onto commercial

July 2026 residential broker renewals near **~4.04% insured 5-year fixed** are a residential benchmark only. Commercial clears on a property package, not a public board.

## Multi-family exception

**5+ unit** rentals often underwrite on NOI/DSCR. Qualifying buildings may use **CMHC MLI Select** for higher LTV and longer amortizations than conventional commercial.
