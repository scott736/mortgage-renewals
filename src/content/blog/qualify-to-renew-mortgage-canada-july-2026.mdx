GSC is full of near-identical questions: **do you need to qualify for a mortgage renewal**, **do you have to qualify to renew**, **do I need to qualify for mortgage renewal**.

## Stay with your lender

If you are current and accept a straight renewal (no new money), most lenders **do not** run a full B-20 re-underwrite. You still must sign the renewal; ignoring it can lead to a less favourable default path.

## Switch lenders

A new lender **does** underwrite you — credit, property, and documentation. Since **November 21, 2024**, many uninsured **straight switches** (same balance, no risky amortization stretch) skip the minimum qualifying rate stress test, but they are not a free pass on credit or docs.

## Refinance / new money

Full qualification and stress test still apply.
