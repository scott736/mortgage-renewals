Search demand for **investment property mortgage rates** and **rental property mortgage rates** is real — but most “rate sites” only publish owner-occupied numbers.

## The investor premium

On 1–4 unit rentals, many Canadian lenders add roughly **0.10%–0.30%+** above the equivalent owner-occupied price. July 2026 broker-channel residential benchmarks sit near **~4.04% insured 5-year fixed** and **~3.35% variable** — treat those as a floor, then add your lender’s rental surcharge.

## How to shop it

1. Get the incumbent rental renewal offer in writing.
2. Ask for owner-occupied vs rental quotes on the same term so the premium is explicit.
3. Confirm you still qualify under rental add-back / offset / DCR rules.
